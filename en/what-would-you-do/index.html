Decision game — five scenarios

What would you do?

History tends to turn on specific decisions by specific people in specific rooms. Here are five real ones. Each is described from the point of view of the person in the room — before they knew how it would end.

After each choice you will see: the consequence of your choice — and what actually happened in history. Your answers are not saved.

Scenario 1 of 5 · May 2021

Take the documents, or leave quietly?

you are a product manager on the Civic Integrity team at Meta

You have worked at Meta for two years. You have access to internal Facebook documents: research showing that Instagram worsens teenage girls' self-image, that the algorithm amplifies polarization, that in Myanmar the platform enables ethnic violence. The internal reports are clear. The company does not publish them, and its public statements contradict what you read in those documents. Your Civic Integrity team has just been dissolved. You have a decision to make.
